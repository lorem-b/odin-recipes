Cartola Recipe

You can't go wrong with cheese and banana

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 50g cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • sugar and cinnamon to taste

Steps

  1. To prep, mix the sugar and the cinnamon in a bowl and peel the banana
  2. In a pan with melted butter, add the banana
  3. After browning it, add the cheesse on top
  4. Add the sugar and cinnamon
