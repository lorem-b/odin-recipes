Sweet Popcorn Recipe

Popcorn but with sugar!

Ingredients

  • 1 tea-cup popcorn kernels
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 5 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp water

Steps

  1. In a pan, add all the ingredients
  2. Mix untill the popcorn kernels start to pop
  3. CLose the pan and stir until there's no more popping
  4. Open the pan and put in a bowl
